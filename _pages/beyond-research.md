> *"What makes happier and healthier humans"* — and a lot of it, for me, happens outdoors.

Outside of economics, I'm a big fan of the outdoors and endurance sport — running, cycling, triathlon, skiing, and hiking — and I love building an active community wherever I am.

## Running

Over the last five years (2021–2025) I've run more than **13,500 km (8,400+ miles)**. Along the way I've completed two **50 km ultramarathons** (2023, 2025), three **marathons** (2024, 2025), and many **half-marathons** (2019–2025).

I've also kept a personal running streak going since **March 2021** — at least 3 km (2 miles) every single day.

## Triathlon and Cycling

I've raced a **Half-Ironman triathlon** (Victoria, BC, 2022) and an **Olympic-distance triathlon** (Vancouver, 2019), and enjoy long rides — including a first 100 km "century" ride.

## Skiing and Hiking

In the mountains I ski (Whistler Blackcomb, Cypress, Sun Peaks) and hike whenever I can — favorites include Garibaldi Lake, Mt. Elphinstone on the Sunshine Coast, Big Bear, and Yosemite.
